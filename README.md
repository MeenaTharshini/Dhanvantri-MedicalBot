## 🌿 Dhanvantri AI – Preventive Health Companion

Dhanvantri AI is a hybrid medical chatbot designed to provide safe, preventive health guidance using a combination of:

🪔 Ayurveda
🌿 Siddha Medicine
🌱 Herbal Remedies
🧘 Yoga & Lifestyle Practices

Tagline: Predict , Prevent , Protect
It blends rule-based medical safety with AI-powered conversation to ensure helpful and responsible guidance.

## ⚙️ Key Features
🩺 Symptom-based Guidance (rule-based safety system)
🤖 AI-powered responses for general queries
🌿 Herbal remedy suggestions
🧘 Yoga & emotional wellness guidance
📚 Ayurveda & Siddha knowledge base
💡 Daily health tips & lifestyle suggestions
🔐 User authentication system (Login/Signup)
💬 Chat history tracking
🧠 How It Works

## Dhanvantri AI follows a hybrid architecture:

✅ Rule-based system
Detects symptoms
Provides safe, structured remedies
Handles emergency cases
🤖 AI system
Handles general and unknown queries
Improves conversational experience

👉 This ensures accuracy + flexibility

## ⚠️ Disclaimer

Dhanvantri AI is a preventive health companion and NOT a replacement for professional medical advice.

All remedies are based on traditional home practices

For serious symptoms, users are always advised to consult a doctor
